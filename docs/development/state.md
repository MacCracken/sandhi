**0.9.10** — 2026-04-25. **Pool stale-skip hardening.** Tightens the 0.9.6 `_sandhi_pool_has_idle` peek to count only conns within `idle_timeout_ms`. Previously the peek returned 1 for any route with stored entries regardless of age, so on a route whose only 1.1 conns had aged past the 90 s idle timeout, ALPN promotion was incorrectly skipped — h2 was never attempted even though those stale entries would be discarded on the next take. Walk is non-destructive (eviction happens naturally on `_sandhi_pool_take`'s stale-skip path); cost is one `clock_now_ms()` per peek plus a vec walk bounded by `max_per_host` (default 8). ADR 0005 freeze respected (no public-surface change). Was tracked as the only "audited and noted, optimization-grade only" finding from the 0.9.9 audit; promoted out of optimization-grade and into a hardening fix because deterministic h2 selection across long client uptimes is the threshold for "worth the patch before fold." **649 assertions green** (482 sandhi + 167 h2; no regression).
